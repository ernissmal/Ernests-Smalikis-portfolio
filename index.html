Autobiography

From a young age, I exhibited a keen interest in technology,
exploring computers and their components.
At the age of 10, my first successful attempt at restoring a worn-out laptop fueled my passion for technology
and inspired me to pursue education in programming.

I embarked on my educational journey at Valmieras tehnikums,
delving into programming and gaining valuable knowledge in computers, servers, and security.
Internships became pivotal moments, allowing me to diagnose and troubleshoot hardware issues
and later venture into professional web development,
creating an AI-powered product showcase.

Despite facing challenges during a web development bootcamp,
I emerged with enhanced skills in Git, CSS, and virtualization tools like SSH, Vagrant, and virtual machines.
My dedication led me to a security guard position at a local casino due to family circumstances.
Still, I continued learning, attending an Accenture bootcamp in iOS development in 2023.

Currently working as a care taker at Rīgas Austrumu Klīniskā Universitātes Slimnīca (RAKUS),
I've learned to be humble, adaptable, and grateful in the face of financial challenges.
This resilience extends beyond work, influencing my perspective on life and the pursuit of a career in web development,
where my interests lie in creating native iOS internet store applications.

The journey, although diverse and filled with challenges,
has instilled in me a profound sense of humility and gratitude,
making me stronger and more resilient with each passing experience.
