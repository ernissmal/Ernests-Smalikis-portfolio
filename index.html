Ernests Smalikis

Web developer & graphic designer

With a dynamic background in marketing, I bring expertise in planning and managing relevant ad campaigns, fostering client relationships, and expanding clientele.

My journey in the tech world started at the age of 16 when I embarked on the path of programming at Valmieras Tehnikums. This passion for technology evolved through Scandiweb's web developer bootcamp, where I gained a deep understanding of professional website concepts, frontend and backend fundamentals, and honed my skills in using tools like Vagrant, Virtual machines, and Ubuntu servers.

My commitment to excellence led me to Accenture's iOS mobile development program, where I delved into creating innovative mobile solutions for iPhones, mastering the fundamentals of app development.

Throughout my educational journey, I applied my knowledge during internships as a computer technician at DP Serviss and as a web developer under the guidance of a self-employed mentor. These experiences have not only shaped my technical skills but have instilled a holistic approach to problem-solving.
